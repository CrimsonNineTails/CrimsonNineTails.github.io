This is the clasic snake game. The Controls: W = up, D = right, S = down, and A = left. This was a project I made in 9th grade at Pasadena High School in the app Academy. This game had many challenges in creation but by far the most challenging part was the death animation. The main issue was that I wasn't quite sure how I wanted to exicute the death animation. I ended up changing the image to and explosion and then making it grow and then dissapear once its reached a certain size.
